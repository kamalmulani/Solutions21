About

Army Institue of Technology, Pune was set in the year 1994 and was formally inaugurated on 24th February, 1995. Our institute runs with a motive to carve the young minds to work with excellence and integrity to acheive a glorious positions in their lives.

One of the step towards "onward to glory" is the amusing, magnificient and splendid event "Solutions 2k21".We are happy that we conducted this event for the past 6 years in a successful way with a great reach. Inspired by the previous years and to acheive the same in this year, we has emerged here with 20+ events in all 6 catagories. Each event fills your heart with fun, enjoyment and satisfaction. Exciting prizes worth 4lac+ awaits for you. Talent should not go in vain. Lots of surprises hang on "18th - 21st May". So, join us and showcase your talents with full enthusiasm.
