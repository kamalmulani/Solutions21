About

Army Institue of Technology, Pune was set in the year 1994 and was formally inaugurated on 24th February, 1995. Our institute runs with a motive to carve the young minds to work with excellence and integrity to acheive a glorious positions in their lives.

One of the step towards "onward to glory" is the amusing, magnificient and splendid event "Solutions 2k21".We are happy that we conducted this event for the past 6 years in a successful way with a great reach. Inspired by the previous years and to acheive the same in this year, we has emerged here with 20+ events in all 6 catagories. Each event fills your heart with fun, enjoyment and satisfaction. Exciting prizes worth 4lac+ awaits for you. Talent should not go in vain. Lots of surprises hang on "18th - 21st May". So, join us and showcase your talents with full enthusiasm.

Events

Your Inner potential cannnot develop without your willingness and conscious participation - Philip Arnold

So here we are gonna provide an eventful platform to unleash your whole potential in Solutions 2k21. At solutions we have events in every expertise, starting from coding to events of gaming, events related to practice of placement Interviews and giving you the idea of startups. So the events in Solutions 2k21 are divided into fields of Coding, Gaming, Mechanical, Electrical, Open Events, Robotics, E-Cell and last but not the least ISDF.

In Coding Field we have events named - Code Red, Bug Off, Reverse Coding and Only Girls coding Event.
Further in Gaming Field we have events named - CODM(BR and MP), Valorant, Clash Royale and Free Fire.
Furthur in Mechanical Field we have events named - Catia and AutoCAD.
Furthur in Electrical Field we have events named - NAND IT and IMPEDENCE.
Furthur in Open Events we have events named - Tech Matrix, Sudoku Tech, Rubix Cube, Photoshop event for Poster, Placement APTI, Ad Making and Photography.
Furthur in Robotics field we have events named - event 1, event 2 and event 3
Furthur in E-Cell field we have events named - event 1, event 2 and event 3
And in ISDF field we have events named - event 1, event 2 and event 3

For furthur details click on the field name you want to know about.
